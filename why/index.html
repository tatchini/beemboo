Why LuckyMile?

We are not obsessed with the ticket sale. Our main goal is to help people throughout their travel. We take care of routine business while providing any support necessary. We handle the process of searching for a good offer, booking and refunding. Most importantly, we find an optimal route with the most appealing price on the market. Up to 60% of money saved – this is not some marketing trick, this is real.

Not a single one of online booking services provides such low costs that we do. We are proud to inform that we have about ten partners around the globe. Together we strive after large discounts for our clients. Where others spend hundreds of thousands dollars per year, customers of LuckyMile spend half as much, or even two times less. Try to imagine those sums of money saved!

We choose only the best and well-known airlines. Business and first class segments equal the highest possible quality of service. Bad airplane condition is the primary reason of customer loss and gaining bad reputation, that’s why we will never let that happen.

Every customer gets individual attention. We keep in mind all the requests and wishes, even minor ones, like food preferences. Moreover we try to suggest precisely what our customer wants. Should you prefer to use the chosen company’s service, we will consider it in the first place.

Our customers are free from the routine. No more endless searching for the optimal and reliable flight. Let our personal managers take care of this! To get their assistance, you only need to leave request on the main page of our site. Our manager will contact you shortly after that and will tell everything you need to know about upcoming travel: how to get to the desired destination in a best way possible, why choose this airline and not the other, where is a smoking room in the airport, and so on.

We respect our regular customers, that’s why they have access to the free Elite Club concierge (or lifestyle management) service. Imagine sending a group of people to Antarctica, organizing a whale photo-hunt or even creating a personal travel guide to the most remote parts of the world LuckyMile will do both possible and impossible to accomplish. Of course members of the club get even more discount.
