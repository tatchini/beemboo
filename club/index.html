Elite Club

Elite Club is a loyalty program for our regular customers. Every participant gets bonus discounts and exclusive offers. For example, one first class flight by price of business class. Every tenth ticket costs half as much for you, and if you book 4 or more tickets, you achieve a bonus discount. These benefits are exclusive for members of the Elite Club.

Club works as a professional concierge service and deals with the organization of any trips, even difficult ones. Our specialists try to fulfill any requests, including even those that are not related to the flight themselves. Arrange a date at the other side of the globe, set up an expedition to Antarctica with penguin photo-hunt, make a route along eastern coast of Africa – all this and more is not a problem for our specialists.

To become a member of our Club, you should fly with LuckyMile at least 20 times. Recommend us to your friends and relatives and join the Club fast!

Every year LuckyMile conducts a party in honor of the Elite Club creation day and invites all the participants. Meet you there!
